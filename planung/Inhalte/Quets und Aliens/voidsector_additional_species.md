# Additional Alien Species -- VoidSector

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# The Scrappers

Overview Nomadic scavengers living in asteroid fields.

Culture They value salvage more than territory.

Gameplay - salvage missions - black market trade

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# The Helion Collective

Overview Energy-based lifeforms living inside stars.

Appearance Plasma structures and light patterns.

Gameplay - stellar research - dangerous energy storms

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# The Silent Swarm

Overview Autonomous machines left behind by an extinct civilization.

Behavior They replicate slowly and defend their territory.

Gameplay - rogue AI encounters - derelict megastructures

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# The Mirror Minds

Overview Telepathic species capable of reading emotions.

Gameplay Diplomatic quests where honesty affects outcomes.
