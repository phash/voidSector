# VoidSector Lore Wiki

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Humanity

Humanity launched the **VoidSector Initiative** believing it would
discover the center of the universe.

Early explorers expected to find:

-   cosmic significance
-   advanced allies
-   limitless resources

Instead they discovered a universe that barely noticed them.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# The VoidSector Initiative

An international program designed to:

-   explore deep space
-   expand human colonies
-   discover alien life

Its explorers are equipped with modular ships capable of mining, combat,
and research.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# The Galactic Reality

Most alien civilizations consider humanity:

-   young
-   loud
-   technologically average

But they also recognize a rare human trait:

creativity under chaos.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# The Myth of the Center

Many young civilizations assume the universe has a center.

Ancient races know the truth:

The universe has no central point of importance.

The search itself is a cultural rite that many species experience.
