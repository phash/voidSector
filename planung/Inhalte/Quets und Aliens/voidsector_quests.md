# VoidSector Quest Collection

This document lists example quests used in the game.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Early Game Quests

## First Signal

A mysterious signal is detected in deep space.

Objectives - Scan signal source - Travel to coordinates - Establish
communication

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Mapping the Unknown

The Archivists request star charts of nearby systems.

Objectives - Scan 5 star systems - Upload cartographic data

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## K'thari Combat Trial

A K'thari commander challenges the player.

Objectives - Survive combat simulation - Defeat K'thari drone fighters

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Mid Game Quests

## The Living Planet

Mycelian lifeforms are spreading across a world.

Objectives - Collect spores - Decide to protect or destroy the ecosystem

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Trade Route Crisis

The Consortium needs an urgent delivery.

Objectives - Transport rare minerals - Avoid pirate ambush

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Lost Expedition

A human exploration fleet vanished.

Objectives - Track distress beacon - Investigate abandoned ships

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Late Game Quests

## The Tourist Attraction

Alien tourists want to observe humans.

Objectives - Escort tourist ship - Demonstrate human culture

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## The Impossible Artifact

An object built by the Axioms appears.

Objectives - Analyze artifact - Solve energy puzzle

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## The Center of the Universe

Players follow clues pointing toward a cosmic location.

Outcome: They discover there is no true center.
